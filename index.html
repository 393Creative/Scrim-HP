Stone Mountain

Stone Mountain Park is a 3,200-acre park located just east of Atlanta, Georgia. The park also features a variety of other attractions,including a cable car,a train ride, a lake, a campground, and a variety of hiking trails. Stone Mountain Park is a popular destination for families, and it offers something for everyone. Visitors can learn about the history of the Stone Mountain, enjoy the Laser Show, or simply relax and have fun.

Helen, Ga

Helen is home to over 200 shops and restaurants, many of which are housed in traditional Bavarian-style buildings. The town also has a number of wineries, breweries, and distilleries. Helen is surrounded by beautiful mountains and forests, and there are many opportunities for outdoor recreation in the area. Visitors can go hiking, biking, fishing, camping, and whitewater rafting. Helen is also a popular destination for Oktoberfest celebrations. The town hosts a week-long festival in October that features traditional German food, beer, and music.
